Empowering Lives through 3D Printed Prosthetics
How the KPC team helped revolutionize the lives of individuals with limb differences and empowering them to embrace a world of new possibilities.
Back
In this case study, we explore the impactful collaboration between New Breed Movement and a dedicated team of innovators. Together, they embarked on a groundbreaking 3D Printed Prosthetics project, revolutionizing the lives of individuals with limb differences and empowering them to embrace a world of new possibilities.

Client Overview:
New Breed Movement is a non-profit organization committed to fostering inclusivity and accessibility for individuals with limb differences. Their mission is to improve the lives of amputees through innovative solutions and community support.
Industry: 3D Prosthetics
Location: South Africa
Client since: 2017
The Challenge:
Traditional prosthetics often come with high costs and limited customization
With the aim of creating affordable and customizable prosthetics, New Breed Movement partnered with our team of engineers and designers to explore the potential of 3D printing technology. Traditional prosthetics often come with high costs and limited customization, making accessibility a challenge for many individuals.
The Solution:
KPC Collaboratively used innovative 3D printing technology
The project team worked collaboratively, leveraging their diverse expertise to create a transformative solution:
Design Innovation: Engineers and designers collaborated closely to develop prosthetic designs that were both functional and aesthetically appealing.
3D Printing Technology: Utilizing advanced 3D printing technology, the team could create prosthetics with intricate designs and a high degree of precision.
Material Selection: Careful consideration was given to selecting materials that were lightweight, durable, and comfortable for daily use.
The Implementation Process:
The implementation process followed a structured approach:
Prototype Development: Several iterations of prosthetic prototypes were designed and tested to ensure optimal fit and functionality.
User Testing and Feedback: The team engaged with amputees to gather valuable feedback, allowing them to fine-tune the prosthetics according to individual preferences.
Community Outreach: New Breed Movement actively engaged with communities to identify individuals in need of prosthetics and provide them with personalized solutions.
The Results:
New Breed Movement has exemplified the transformative potential of 3D printing technology.
The 3D Printed Prosthetics project achieved remarkable results and left a lasting impact:
Affordable Solutions: The 3D printed prosthetics were significantly more cost-effective than traditional alternatives, making them accessible to a wider audience.
Personalized Support: Each amputee received a customized prosthetic that fit their unique needs, enhancing comfort and mobility.
Empowering Lives: The project had a profound effect on the lives of recipients, allowing them to embrace newfound independence and opportunities.
KPC's support redefined possibilities in our 3D Printed Prosthetics project. With their expertise, we empowered lives with affordable, customized solutions for amputees.
